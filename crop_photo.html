How to Crop Your Photo Properly:

  1. Open the original photo in any image editing software (Paint, Photoshop, GIMP, or online tools like Canva)
  2. Crop to focus on your face - make sure your face is clearly visible and centered
  3. Use a square aspect ratio (1:1) since the portfolio uses a circular display
  4. Include your head and shoulders for the best professional look
  5. Save as JPG with good quality (around 500x500 pixels is perfect)
  6. Replace the current MyPhoto.jpg with your cropped version
